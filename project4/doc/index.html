Introduction

This project is inspired by climate change and the melting of ice caps. I am trying to simulate the monitoring system in nature - record the process of glacier melting.

[Inspiration] Glacier Monitoring System

[Visual Reference] Moebius

I created a glacier monitoring interface using p5.js. I try to make it visualize the recording of glaciers melting in real-time. Users can adjust the speed and pause the "recording" anytime.

Work Process

Core Setting & Process

  • Draw and color setting - drawPeak function: In order to give users a sense of real environment, I draw glaciers in multiple layers and use gradient colors. Each layer's color transitions as the melting progresses, reflecting different stages of a day.
  • Melting mechanism - meltPeak function: glaciers will melt as calculated.
  • Control buttons - speedbutton & pausebutton: 5 buttons lets users play in different spped, pause and resume the simulation, mimicking the ability to analyze snapshots of data in real-life monitoring.
  • Small monitoring window - drawMonitor function: I draw a graphical "monitor" on corner of the interface visualizes the remaining surface area of the glacier, providing an abstracted overview of the simulation's progress.
